Asuka Basics:

Best Normals:

c.S (Close Slash)

Asuka's only normal that is advantageous on block. Poor range, but useful in combos.

f.S (Far Slash)

One of Asuka's best pokes due to its speed, range, and safe hurtbox.

5H (Standing Heavy)

Asuka's farthest reaching normal. Great for controlling space in neutral.

6K/6H

Asuka's standing Low and Overhead respectively. Though they are slow, they can be catch the opponent off guard when combined with spells.

Special Moves (Non-Spells):

The only special moves Asuka has are related to his spell mechanic. Imagine you have 3 decks or "Test Cases" of cards or "Spells." You have 4 slots that you can draw cards into from a specific deck. For example: If Test Case 2 is active, you cannot draw a card from Test Case 3.

Chant

The move that allows Asuka to cast Spells. The button pressed determines slot used.

Bookmark

Draws a new spell into Asuka's slots.

Recover Mana

Recovers Mana, can choose to recover slowly, or quickly while draining health or meter.

Change Test Case

Changes the "Deck" that Asuka draws from.
